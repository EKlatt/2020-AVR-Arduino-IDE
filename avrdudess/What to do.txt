This IDE runs within the Windows-Shell.
The IDE-folder ..\MyIDE\avrdudess contains a "gui" for avrdude "avrdudess.exe" derived from:
https://blog.zakkemble.net/avrdudess-a-gui-for-avrdude/
so the avrdude gui can be called within the IDE.

1. Download: https://blog.zakkemble.net/download/AVRDUDESS-2.13-portable.zip
2. Unpack: AVRDUDESS-2.13-portable.zip
3. Copy files in AVRDUDESS-2.13-portable to ..\MyIDE\avrdudess
