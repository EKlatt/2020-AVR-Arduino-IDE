This IDE runs within the Windows-Shell.
The IDE-folder ..\MyIDE\avr_sim shall contain Gerd's AVR Simulator derived from:
http://www.avr-asm-tutorial.net/avr_sim/index_de.html
So the AVR simulator can be called within the IDE

1. Download: http://www.avr-asm-tutorial.net/avr_sim/23/avr_sim_23_win64_debug.zip
2. Unpack: avr_sim_23_win64_debug.zip
3. Copy avr_sim.exe to ..\MyIDE\avr_sim
